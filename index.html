MRIS-51 GURUGRAM

Hi!

Manav Rachna International School-51, Gurugram, is a co-educational progressive school which takes responsibility to prepare every child for the global world. We take pride in our international standards of teaching which focuses on the latest technology driven curriculum, experiential learning, and child-centric teaching strategies. MRIS-51 is the only school in which exposes students to Virtual Reality from Grade I onwards. We sensitize students about various cultures of the world through exchange programs. We strive to make them global citizens, and thus, instill essential life skills in them and counsel them regarding their career goals from Grade VII onwards. Our holistic development approach includes research based learning through Annual Projects based on United Nations Goals. We endeavour to promote ethical behaviour and social skills in our children through Soft Skills program. The school practices inclusive education with an active Special Education Department which caters to the needs of special children. Our school strives to develop the 3 C’s- Creativity-Collaboration-Critical Thinking amongst students. We ensure transparency by making parents our equal partners in their child’s education and teachers as facilitators. MRIS-51 is a happy and contended family of students, parents and educators who thrive in this playground of education.
